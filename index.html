The Future Is Now!

AfroCon is an inclusive celebration of nerd culture that embraces boundless creativity. It’s a platform for passionate fans of diverse genres, supports talented independent artists and businesses led by people of color. AfroCon is where conversations ignite, our powers combine, and magical things happen!


Join us at AfroCon for an unforgettable experience! Attend captivating panels, discover exclusive merchandise from unique vendors, and connect with fellow enthusiasts. Don’t miss out on this epic event that celebrates diversity, creativity, and the power of fandom. Get your tickets now and be part of the magic at AfroCon!
